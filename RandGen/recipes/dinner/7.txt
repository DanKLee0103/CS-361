Spaghetti Carbonara 🍝:
    Ingredients:
        Spaghetti
        Pancetta or bacon
        Eggs
        Parmesan cheese
        Black pepper

    Instructions:
        Cook spaghetti until al dente.
        Sauté pancetta or bacon until crispy.
        Beat eggs, Parmesan cheese, and black pepper together.
        Toss cooked pasta with egg mixture and pancetta.
        Serve immediately.

    Calories: Approximately 500 per serving
