    Ingredients:
        Fresh mozzarella
        Tomatoes
        Fresh basil leaves
        Balsamic glaze
        Olive oil
        Salt and pepper

    Instructions:
        Slice the fresh mozzarella and tomatoes into rounds.
        Arrange them alternately on a serving plate, overlapping slightly.
        Place fresh basil leaves between the slices.
        Drizzle extra-virgin olive oil over the mozzarella and tomatoes.
        Follow with a generous drizzle of balsamic glaze for a sweet and tangy flavor.
        Sprinkle a pinch of salt and freshly ground black pepper over the salad.
        Serve immediately as an appetizer or side dish.
        Enjoy the vibrant flavors of this classic Italian salad!

    Calories: Approximately 200 per serving
