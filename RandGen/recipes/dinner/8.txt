Salmon with Lemon-Dill Sauce üêü:
    Ingredients:
        Salmon fillets
        Lemon juice
        Fresh dill
        Olive oil
        Salt and pepper

    Instructions:
        Season salmon with salt and pepper.
        Grill or bake until cooked through.
        Mix lemon juice, chopped dill, and olive oil for the sauce.
        Drizzle sauce over salmon.
        Serve with steamed asparagus or roasted potatoes.

    Calories: Approximately 350 per serving
